Welcome to the YatrAmore Global Lucky Draw! By participating in the contest, you agree to the following rules and conditions.

1. Eligibility & Registration

  • One Entry Per Person: Only one registration per person is allowed. Multiple entries using different emails or devices will lead to disqualification.
  • Age Requirement: You must be 18 years or older to participate in the draw.
  • Staff & Moderators Exclusion: YatrAmore employees, moderators, affiliates, and their immediate family members are strictly prohibited from participating in the draw to ensure absolute fairness.

2. Mandatory Social Media Requirements

To be eligible to claim your prize, the winner must be actively following us on our official social media channels. Verification will be performed before the prize is awarded:

  • Instagram: Must follow our official page. Follow us here.
  • TikTok: Must follow our TikTok page (Exempted for residents of India where TikTok is unavailable). Follow us here.
  • YouTube: Must be subscribed to our YouTube channel. Subscribe here.

3. The Draw Process

  • Frequency: The draw will be conducted once every 60 days.
  • Selection: The winner will be chosen through a completely random, automated selection process from the pool of valid registrations.

4. The Prize (Discount Codes)

  • Discount Codes: The winner will be provided with exclusive YatrAmore discount codes. The specific details and value of the available discount codes will be provided to the winner upon claiming the prize.
  • Geographical Limitations: Please note that depending on your geographical location or region, certain discount codes or partner offers may be unavailable, restricted, or unredeemable.
  • Non-Transferable: Prizes are strictly non-transferable, non-exchangeable, and cannot be redeemed for cash.

5. Winner Announcement & Privacy

  • Winner Wall: The winner's name will be proudly displayed on the YatrAmore Lucky Draw Winner Wall.
  • Anonymity: If requested, the winner has the right to remain anonymous on the public Winner Wall to protect their privacy.

6. Right to Cancel

Strict Compliance: If any of the above criteria (including the mandatory social media requirements and one-entry limit) are not met, or if fraudulent activity is suspected, YatrAmore reserves the absolute right to cancel the winner's prize and draw a new winner.
