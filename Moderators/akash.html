Hello everyone! Greetings from India. I am a cardiovascular researcher and pharmacologist. I am delighted to introduce myself and share how I became part of the YatrAmore family.

My journey with YatrAmore began when I was preparing for my trip to Italy to attend the European Society of Cardiology (ESC) Congress. Since it was my first time traveling there, I was searching for reliable travel advice and local insights. While browsing Instagram, I came across the YatrAmore page. Their content immediately stood out not only because they shared practical travel experiences but also because of the warmth, positivity, and genuine connection they shared with people from different cultures.

I decided to reach out to them for guidance regarding my trip. To my surprise, they responded with incredible kindness and were genuinely willing to help. What started as a simple conversation about travel soon became much more. Their friendliness, humility and welcoming nature made me feel like I was talking to old friends rather than content creators.

From that moment, I continued following their journey and became part of the amazing YatrAmore community. Beyond travel, I admire their mission of bringing people together, celebrating different cultures, and inspiring others to explore the world with an open mind and heart. Today, I am proud to support the YatrAmore project and be part of such a positive and caring community. I am grateful that a search for travel advice before an important scientific conference led me to meet such wonderful people. I look forward to sharing many more experiences with this incredible family. Thank you for welcoming me into the YatrAmore family!
