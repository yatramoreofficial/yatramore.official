About Us

We are Laura and Yazavinder, the couple behind YatrAmore. Yazavinder, also known as Yaza, is from Jammu and Kashmir, India, and Laura is from Veneto, Italy. Together, we share our journey as an Indo-Italian couple through love, culture, travel, and everyday life.
YatrAmore is our project and creative space where we share what it looks like when two different cultures come together. Through our story, we want to show the beauty, fun, challenges, and warmth of building a life across backgrounds, traditions, and experiences.
The name YatrAmore brings together two beautiful words from two different cultures. “Yatra” means journey or travel in Hindi and Punjabi, while “Amore” means love in Italian. Together, YatrAmore reflects a journey of love.
We are both 31 years old and enjoying this beautiful phase of life together. Every year brings new memories, new lessons, and new reasons to keep building YatrAmore.
Yaza is Sikh, and Laura is Christian. Our different faith backgrounds are part of what makes our journey unique, and we respect and appreciate each other’s traditions.
We met through common friends. What started as a simple connection gradually became something special and meaningful for both of us.
Laura works as a Financial Manager, and Yaza is a Researcher. Alongside our professional lives, we also enjoy building YatrAmore and sharing our journey together.

Our Content
