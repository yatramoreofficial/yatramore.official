2. What personal data we collect

Depending on how you interact with YatrAmore, we may collect the following personal data:

A. Contact Form

When you submit a contact enquiry, we collect:

  • Your email address (verified via a one-time code sent to your inbox)
  • The subject and content of your message
  • A unique Device ID (automatically generated and stored locally in your browser to identify your device)
  • Technical Browser Information (User Agent) — used for diagnostic and technical support purposes to ensure compatibility with your specific browser and OS
  • A non-personal System Fingerprint — a technical hash used to enforce rate limits and prevent abuse

B. Family Tree (YA-Family)

If you choose to "Grow a Leaf" on our community banyan tree, we collect:

  • The name you choose to display (alias allowed)
  • Your email address (used only for identity verification and leaf management)
  • Your reflection or personal comment
  • A unique Device ID (automatically generated to help you find your leaf later)
  • Technical Browser Information (User Agent) — used for layout rendering and cross-device leaf support
  • A non-personal System Fingerprint — a technical hash derived from your device's hardware characteristics to prevent duplicate registrations

C. Data Submission & Clearance Portal

When you submit information for publication via our hidden portal, we collect:

  • Your legal full name and public pen name
  • Your email address (for internal communication and verification)
  • Any additional text, digital data, or information you provide
  • Your explicit consent regarding data ownership, our right to edit/format, liability, and your right to request data removal

3. Why we collect your data

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To receive, review, and reply to your enquiry or message
  • To process, review, and format data submitted for publication on YatrAmore via our Submission Portal
  • To verify your email address via a one-time verification code (OTP) before accepting a contact submission
  • To prevent spam and abuse through device-level rate limiting (maximum 2 messages per hour per device) and contest entry limits (using Device ID and secure digital fingerprinting)
  • To maintain a trusted identity token on your device for up to 20 days, so you are not required to re-verify your email on subsequent visits from the same device
  • To facilitate entry, manage eligibility, and administer the Grand Lucky Draw contest, including distributing discount codes
  • To publicly announce the Grand Lucky Draw winner on our Winner Wall (winners may request anonymity to protect their privacy)
  • To verify mandatory contest eligibility requirements, which may involve requesting and reviewing authenticated screenshots of your logged-in social media accounts and analyzing public profile follow history.

We do not use the information submitted through the contact form for marketing purposes unless we clearly ask for your separate consent.

4. Legal basis for processing

In accordance with applicable European data protection rules, we process your personal data for the purpose of responding to your enquiry based on our legitimate interest in managing communications received through the website and, where applicable, on taking steps at your request before entering into a possible collaboration or other communication.

5. How your data is processed

Your data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently and only to the extent necessary for the purpose stated above.

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Where your data is sent

When you submit the contact form, your message may be processed through third-party technical services used to operate the website and receive form submissions.

In particular, contact form data may be transmitted through Google Apps Script and related Google services used to manage incoming enquiries. These service providers may process data on our behalf in accordance with their own legal and contractual obligations.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data received through the contact form only for as long as necessary to manage and respond to your enquiry and for any reasonable follow-up period.

If continued retention is required by law or necessary to protect legal rights, we may retain the data for a longer period where justified.

8. Who may access your data

Your personal data will be accessed only by the persons responsible for managing YatrAmore communications and by service providers involved in the technical operation of the contact form and related tools. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

9. Bot Protection & Anti-Spam (CAPTCHA)

This website uses Cloudflare Turnstile to protect our contact and registration forms from automated spam bots. Turnstile is a privacy-first, invisible CAPTCHA solution that verifies the user is human without requiring them to solve interactive puzzles.

By using our forms, you acknowledge that certain technical data (such as browser information and IP address) may be processed by Cloudflare to provide this security service, in accordance with their privacy standards.

10. Your rights

If you are located in the European Union, including Italy, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, where applicable
  • Request restriction of processing, where applicable
  • Object to processing, where applicable
  • Request data portability, where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: contact@yatramore.com

11. Cookies, local storage, and analytics

This website uses cookies and local storage to ensure functionality and to serve personalized advertisements.

Google AdSense & Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display ads on this site. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to store:

  • A unique Device ID (to identify your device for security purposes and enforce contest rules, such as 60-day lockouts)
  • A trusted identity token (valid for 20 days, allowing verified users to skip re-verification)
  • Your registration status for promotions (e.g., Lucky Draw) to manage eligibility on your device
  • Your theme preference (light/dark mode)

These values are stored entirely within your browser and are never shared with third parties. You can clear them at any time by clearing your browser data.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
