"A guardian on social media and a big sister forever"

Hello there! Greetings from Romanian soil, I am Toxick, and I am delighted to be here. I have just a few passions, but I am deeply committed to them. One is rollerblading, a sport I practice that also helps me lead a healthy, active life. Secondly, I love playing PvP video games; they help me relax and escape the daily work routine we all have to deal with.

My journey with Yatramore began during a rather delicate time in my life. Like everyone else, I am used to social media, and while scrolling through my feed, I was captivated by content from the lovely couple, Laura and Yaza. Upon checking their profile, I discovered so much more; I was pleasantly surprised and decided to follow them, fascinated by the bond between the two of them and the connection between their vastly different cultures.

One day, I received a notification that the couple I adored was hosting a live stream. I joined without hesitation, and though I felt a bit shy about writing to them, I interacted with Laura and Yaza. I was amazed by how incredibly sociable, warm, and welcoming they were; I will never forget that first interaction. From that day on, I decided to stand by this wonderful couple, acting as a guardian for them on social media and a big sister forever. I am happy and proud to be part of the Yatramore project and to know that there are loving, sociable, and trustworthy people out there in the world.
