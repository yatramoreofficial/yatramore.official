Discover YatrAmore

The name YatrAmore brings together two beautiful ideas: "Yatra," a word associated with journey or travel in Hindi and Punjabi, and "Amore," the Italian word for love. Together, it expresses a journey of love — a name that reflects travel, emotion, cultural connection, and the meeting of two identities.

About Us

We are Laura and Yazavinder, the couple behind YatrAmore. Coming from Italian and Indian Sikh backgrounds, we created this space to share our life together through culture, travel, conversations, and the everyday moments that shape our story.

YatrAmore is where we document our experiences honestly and personally — from discovering places and traditions to building a life between different worlds. Through it, we want to share something real, warm, and human.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage openness, understanding, and appreciation for the beauty of different cultures coming together. Through YatrAmore, we hope to show that connection can grow through curiosity, respect, and the willingness to learn from one another.

We want our content to leave people with a sense of closeness, perspective, and positivity — whether they relate to cross-cultural relationships, love, travel, or simply the joy of discovering life through someone else’s eyes.
