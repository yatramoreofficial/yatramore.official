✦ Community Moderators ✦
A journey made of love, discovery, and daily care for one another

We are Sara and Fabrizio, partners in life and digital "guardians" of the YatrAmore community. We live in our beautiful Sardinia, where we share a passion for exploring the human mind, a deep curiosity about the world, and a visceral love for good food and conviviality.

In our duo, Sara is the precise and analytical one. With a degree in Languages and Markets, she works for a firm specializing in rigging and sailboats, though her heart has always beat for psychology and literature. As a true Virgo, she thrives on mental order and precision. Yet, beneath this structured nature lies the heart of an HSP (Highly Sensitive Person): Sara experiences life with a profound emotional intensity, a visceral ocean of feelings that connects her deeply to spirituality, the natural world, and the energy of things. She is also deeply passionate about crystals, tarot, and the world of divination; in fact, given her bond with energy and ancient wisdom, she would have undoubtedly been a wise witch in another era!

Fabrizio is the voice and the smile of our couple. An actor, voice-over artist, and radio speaker, he also brings with him extensive experience in the restaurant industry and in pastry-making. He possesses a natural sense of empathy and a contagious positivity. Currently a Psychology student at university, Fabrizio offers the community his rarest gifts: a sense of lightheartedness that always sparks laughter, and the art of listening. This inner balance and disciplined spirit were forged through twenty years of practicing martial arts, specifically Judo and Kung Fu, which taught him the value of patience and focus. A natural diplomat who avoids conflict, he always prefers the path of constructive dialogue, showing his affection through daily presence and care for others. When he isn’t studying or performing, you’ll likely find him on his Harley Davidson.

What truly binds us together are the passions that nourish the spirit. We both share a deep love for art and music: Fabrizio is a baritone, while Sara has studied singing and loves to paint. There is also a powerful experience that has shaped our sense of responsibility: we both served as emergency first responders (118), a journey that taught us the true value of being there for others in times of need. Today, we share our home with our very special extended family: our dog Agata, a 27 kg bundle of sweetness, and our two long-haired cats, Moon and Desirée.

We believe deeply in the power of human connection and mutual respect. We are fascinated by the unknown and by the beauty of different cultures. We embraced this cause because it mirrors our own philosophy of life: a journey (Yatra) made of love (Amore), discovery, and daily care for one another. We are here to ensure that this space remains a safe harbor for civil dialogue and positivity for everyone.
