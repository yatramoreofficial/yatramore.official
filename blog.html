Our Stories

Welcome to our journal of reflections on travel, cross-cultural love, van life, and everything in between. Through our stories, we aim to capture the raw, unfiltered essence of our adventures around the world. Whether it is a deep dive into the spiritual roots of an ancient city, the quiet beauty of waking up in a van surrounded by nature, or the humorous misunderstandings of a multicultural relationship, we write from the heart.

Our goal is not just to showcase beautiful destinations, but to tell the deeply human stories behind them. We hope these pages inspire you to step out of your comfort zone, embrace different cultures, and perhaps embark on an adventure of your own. Dive into our latest entries below, and travel alongside us as we document the chapters of our lives.
